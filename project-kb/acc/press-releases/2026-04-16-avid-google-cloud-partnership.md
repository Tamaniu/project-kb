# Avid and Google Cloud Announce Partnership to Bring Agentic AI to Media Production

**Source:** Avid Press Room
**Date:** 16/04/2026
**Location:** Burlington, MA and Sunnyvale, CA

> Strategic collaboration integrates Gemini models and Vertex AI into Avid Media Composer and
> Avid Content Core to automate time-intensive film and TV post-production tasks.

---

## ⚠️ Key Fact — Avid Content Core General Availability

**Avid Content Core is now commercially available (GA) as of this announcement (16/04/2026).**
Prior resource centre articles (November 2025) described Content Core in future/planning terms.
This press release confirms the platform has reached general availability.

---

## Partnership Overview

Avid and Google Cloud announced a **multi-year strategic partnership** to integrate generative and agentic AI into Avid's creative tools. The integration targets two products:

- **Media Composer** — industry-standard NLE for professional film and TV
- **Avid Content Core** — cloud-native SaaS platform acting as a unified intelligent data layer for global media assets

Google Cloud technologies being embedded: **Gemini models**, **Vertex AI**, **BigQuery**, **Vision Warehouse**, **Vertex AI Search**.

---

## Key Announcements

### 1. Avid Content Core — General Availability
Now commercially available as a cloud-native SaaS platform. By leveraging Google Cloud's BigQuery, Vision Warehouse, and Vertex AI Search, Content Core transforms passive storage into an active, searchable library — allowing global creative teams to access and manage video files from anywhere.

### 2. Gemini Integration in Media Composer
A multimodal extension powered by Gemini has been added to Media Composer. Capabilities include:
- Intelligent metadata enhancement
- Automated logging
- B-Roll generation
- Reduction of manual burden on creative teams

### 3. Agentic Search and Discovery (both platforms)
Using Vertex AI and multimodal Gemini models, both platforms can understand the context of every file. Users can describe what they need using natural language — based on visual actions, dialogue, or emotional cues — converting what previously required weeks of manual archive discovery into seconds of automated insight.

---

## Agentic AI Capabilities

The partnership enables **agentic AI workflows** — digital assistants capable of autonomously managing complex tasks, including:
- Matching visual styles across footage
- Identifying emotional cues in raw footage
- Streamlining metadata logging
- Querying content using natural language

These are described as the foundation for future agentic integrations across Avid's broader portfolio.
