# The Heart of Avid Content Core

**Source:** Avid Resource Center — <https://www.avid.com/resource-center/avid-content-core>
**Published:** 17/11/2025

---

## Industry Context

The media industry faces several converging pressures: fierce competition for audiences and advertisers; demand for greater efficiency and collaboration across geographically distributed teams; and the need to maximise the revenue-generating potential of content across all platforms.

The post-Covid production boom has generated a long tail of consequences as that volume of content moves through the distribution chain and the burst of streaming platforms moves from a flood to a trickle.

> "There's a lack of visibility and transparency when you're looking at the cost of creating content, cost of distribution of content, and ultimately the performance of that content in the overall space."
> — **Guillaume Aubuchon, Avid VP of Product Management**

---

## What is Avid Content Core?

Avid Content Core is positioned as the industry's first cloud-hosted **Content Data Platform (CDP)** for media production and analysis. It is distinct from — but inclusive of — MAM (Media Asset Management) and PAM (Production Asset Management) capabilities.

> "This is a true content data platform. This is not just a MAM and a PAM. It'll have those pieces in it, but this is really a single pane of glass that allows you to track assets from their origination in the workflow and their creation all the way through distribution and ultimately the performance and monetization of those assets as well."
> — **Guillaume Aubuchon, Avid VP of Product Management**

---

## Key Capabilities

### Cloud Hosting and Hybrid Workflows
- Cloud-hosted, running on **AWS**
- Supports hybrid workflows combining cloud with on-premises solutions
- Integrates with: MediaCentral Production Asset Management, MediaCentral Asset Management, Wolftech News, iNEWS (MediaCentral Newsroom Management), and non-Avid shared storage platforms

### Semantic Search and AI-Driven Discovery
- Indexes content and enables semantic search to reduce the time taken to find the right content for the right story
- Behaviour-aware: learns from user role and workflow context to pre-stage relevant content, reducing active search

> "We have semantic search in the system, but first and foremost, we know enough about the user based on their behavior and their role in the company. And specifically, because we have the workflow portion of it, we know what they do, what they need to do. We can pre-stage those elements for them. So, they're not searching, they're just going right to work."
> — **Guillaume Aubuchon, Avid VP of Product Management**

### Open API Platform
- Open API first: every user action available via API
- No-code and low-code automation interfaces planned
- Agentic AI workflows planned

> "Avid Content Core is an open API platform first and foremost. Every user action you can take into the system, you'll have full API access to as an end user. When you combine that with the ability to have a no code interface where we do not have to be involved in the integration work, you can really take control of your own destiny when it comes to integrating and interoperating."
> — **Guillaume Aubuchon, Avid VP of Product Management**

### Asset Lifecycle Tracking and Monetisation
- Unique **asset fingerprinting** enables tracking from point of creation through distribution
- Full production lifecycle visibility: creation → distribution → performance → monetisation

## Positioning Summary

Avid describes Avid Content Core as a paradigm shift in how companies view their production pipelines:

> "It's a whole new paradigm shift when it comes to being able to interoperate and integrate systems together."
> — **Guillaume Aubuchon, Avid VP of Product Management**
