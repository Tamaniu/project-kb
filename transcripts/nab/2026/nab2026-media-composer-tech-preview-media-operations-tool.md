# 🎞️ MEDIA COMPOSER TECH PREVIEW — Media Operations Tool

Another cool tool that's coming later
this year in Media Composer is a media
operations tool. Media operations tool
gives me the capabilities of putting
things into the background to be
processed, whether it's a transcode,
whether it's a export, whether it's a
mix down, or whether it's a render.
Gives the editor the capabilities to
continue working. So, those days of
coffee breaks are gone, editors.
So, I can go export, I can click here, I
can actually click there, pick the
format, hit start, and this will all
happen and it'll give me the
capabilities of continuing my job in
editing.
