# Language Management

## Overview

Wolftech supports multiple languages to enable teams around the world to work in their preferred language.

## Setting Your Language Preference

Users can set their preferred language in the My Page settings section. Navigate to Settings and select your language from the available options.

## Localisation Support

Wolftech provides comprehensive localisation support including:
- User interface translations
- Date and time format localisation
- Number and currency format support
- Timezone management

## Custom Language Configuration

Organisations can customise language settings and add custom terminology specific to their operations. Please contact your administrator to discuss custom language requirements.

**Note:** Your organisation will likely have a custom language so the system speaks your operational way of talking. Please don't change the language without confirming with your admin.
