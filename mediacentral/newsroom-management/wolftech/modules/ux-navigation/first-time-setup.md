# User Initial Setup and Help

## My Page Setup

As you are welcomed into the platform you will want to set your profile up. Navigate to the My Page section from the top right menu.

Here you can update your:

### Profile
- Change your profile picture
- Update your preferred name
- Select your default department or brand (it doesn't have much consequence if you work across multiples but this will become your shortcut default when creating new productions, you can always change it manually each time)
- Set the office you are currently working from (as opposed to the offices field, which lists the offices you operate in, the currently working from office is your base office)
- Select your default calendar (this is currently not in use but future facing) and you can always view other calendars but it can be the default calendar listing events
- Add a location so people can find you (this is optional and it can be your working office address). It will help people understand where you are located when collaborating globally
- Change your password for accounts not controlled via SSO login

### Settings
Here you can adjust:
- The language to your spoken language
- Define the clock type you use (12 or 24hs)
- Select your display comfort mode experience (High contrast, wide letter spacing and large target areas)
- Manage private mention groups. These are easy groups that anyone can define to speak to a number of users at once with an @mention

**Note:** Your organisation will likely have a custom language so the system speaks your operational way of talking. Please don't change the language without confirming with your admin.

### Notifications
Set up your notifications preferences (In app, Web, emails, mobile etc.)

**Note:** Web browser notification will require you to enable them in Chrome. Some notifications may be enforced by the organisation to always come in a format and always reach all users. Reach out to your administrator for further details.
