# Read-In Notes (Logs)

## Intro

Read-In Notes are like a sibling to a story or an episode that allows you to maintain a log or notes section. A Log can be marked as a Private log. Simply select the option when you create them.

Read-In notes can be created from the quick add menu. As opposed to Stories, the read-in notes don't require publishing points or as much detail as a story or production typically would.

## Overview

Logs are like a sibling to a story or an episode that allow you to add notes to the Notes section. Logs can be private. Simply select the option when you create them. 

Logs include a chat component where team members can collaborate and discuss content related to the log.

**Note:** You cannot drag a log after the fact to a story to link them directly.
