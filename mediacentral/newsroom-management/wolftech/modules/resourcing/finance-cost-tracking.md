# Resourcing: Finance – Cost Tracking

## Cost Tracking

As you begin adding resources to your story, people with finance permissions can begin tracking costs against it. At this point all of your cost tracking information is available in the finance section.

Costs can be tracked at multiple levels:
- Story-level cost tracking
- Resource-level costs (based on rates and resource types)
- Project budgets and budget codes

When resources are booked to stories or productions, their associated costs are automatically calculated and rolled up for financial reporting and budget management purposes.

## Finance Permissions

Finance permissions determine who can view, create, and manage cost tracking information within stories and productions. Administrators can set granular permissions for different user groups to control access to sensitive financial data.
