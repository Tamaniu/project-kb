# Shoots and Location Work

## Intro

As you develop your productions and plan shoots, it may be important to define where you plan to shoot your story or production.

## Setting a Location

Adding a location to your story or production allows teams to track where shoots will take place, manage logistics, and assess any location-based risks.

## Map Overview

The map view allows you to search for and visualise available resources based on location. You can filter by what you are looking for (Crew, Guest, Equipment, Teams, Points of Interest or Stories/Productions).

## Distance to Location

With the map opened if you select a story from the left menu and select a resource from the map you will be able to select the show distance button which will tell you the path, distance and time it will take that resource to get to the location by car.

## Risk Assessment

Location-based risk assessments can be conducted to identify potential hazards or challenges associated with shooting in particular locations. This allows teams to plan appropriately and ensure crew safety.
