# Resourcing: Bookings

## Overview

Resourcing (Bookings, Requests, Guest Overview, Finance and Tracking) is a major part of the Wolftech application allowing you to have clarity on who is doing what when as well as driving better collaboration across teams.

## Story Bookings

### Story Booking Page

In the second tab of the story you will find the area for bookings. Here you can see Booking grouped under:
- Bookings for the story
- Bookings associated with a particular location (Note, you may need a location risk assessment in place to confirm them)
- Bookings for a particular publishing point

On the right you will be able to search resources and book them.

### Adding Contacts

This action will load the list of guests (contacts) on the right to choose from. When you click add it will add a contact card as reference to the story. Note this is not a booking per se but adding the profile to the story so you know that contact is associated with the story but is not booked on a given day or time.

Note this does not add a reference of the story to the profile booking history as it is not a booking.

### Direct Bookings

Here you will be able to book people or equipment to the story. It will load your resources on the right from where you can book guests (contacts), employees, freelancers, vendors or equipment.

#### Finding the Right Resource

- Use the side buttons on the right to choose categories (Staff for employees and freelancers, technical for equipment, teams or guests)
- Use the search bar to find someone in particular
- Leverage the filters to refine your search by resource type, office and more
- Use your recently used resources
- Or navigate through the resource types sections

Finally if you don't find the resource you may want to create a profile for the freelancer or guest that is not listed and you can do so via the + button below.
