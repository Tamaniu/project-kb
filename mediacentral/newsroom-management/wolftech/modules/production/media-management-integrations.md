# Media Management & Integrations

## Intro

Wolftech comes with rich Media workflows that are deeply integrated into your workflows enabling journalists and teams to focus on their stories while complex systems and media flows simply follow them.

At Wolftech we have a best of breed approach allowing organisations to plug their preferred solutions across cameras, transmitters, media asset management systems and any other systems that need to integrate along the production and distribution cycle.

In this spirit, we have established a common media framework to simplify your integrations and speed up your team's onboarding because we believe swapping systems should be much easier.

For more information get in touch through your solutions specialist to discuss how to get started.

The system is very configurable to enable the setup of the right workflow for each part of the organization and its continued evolution over time as your organization grows and evolves.

## Summary

In summary wolftech stories have:

- A **Board** where assets, links, documents, notes, and references from any 3rd party connected MAM can be stored inside of your story or your publishing output. It acts as the central source of truth for all of your story media.
- A **Hunter** module to search connected PAMs, MAMs and Wolftech elements and link them to your story
- A **Media Folder** panel where you can preview the story connected MAM folder (Note items in the board can be ingested into the PAM, MAM so it can act as a gate for compliance before ingest)
- A **Connected Devices** panel for connected cameras, transmitters, etc.
- A **Footage** panel for previewing connected Camera cards

Wolftech runs most of its integrations through a powerful workflow engine that can communicate with both cloud and on-premises systems through an SQS/SNS Queue making it easy to continue to do changes in the future without difficult security processes each time around.
