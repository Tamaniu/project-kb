# Fact Checking and Content Authenticity

## Overview

Fact Checking and Content Authenticity workflows enable editorial teams to verify information and track content provenance throughout the production process.

## Content Authenticity

Wolftech has partnered with industry standards bodies to offer workflows that determine content provenance of files added to the board, helping teams verify the authenticity and origin of media assets.

## Verification Workflows

The fact-checking system enables:
- Systematic verification of claims and sources
- Tracking of fact-check status throughout production
- Collaboration between editorial and fact-checking teams
- Audit trails for verification processes

## Integration with Editorial Process

Fact-checking workflows are integrated into the editorial segments and guidance systems, allowing teams to request reviews and track verification status before publication.
