# Editorial Background Information

## Overview

Background Information (Editorial Development) provides editorial teams with comprehensive context and research materials for story development and production planning.

This feature allows teams to:
- Gather and organise editorial research
- Document story development processes
- Maintain background information for complex stories
- Track editorial development history

## Background Information Segments

Editorial segments can include:
- Research notes and sources
- Story development timeline
- Context and historical background
- Key facts and figures
- Interview notes and transcripts

## Managing Background Information

Background information can be attached to stories and productions to ensure the entire team has access to essential editorial context during the production process.
