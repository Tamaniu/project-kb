# Content Guidance

## Overview

Content Guidance allows users to request expert review and approval from specialist teams including legal, rights and clearances (R&C), Standards & Practices (S&P), and risk management personnel before content is published.

## Guidance Workflows

The guidance system enables organisations to:
- Request expert review of sensitive content
- Track approval workflows for compliance
- Maintain audit trails for regulatory requirements
- Collaborate between editorial and compliance teams

## Accessing Content Guidance

Content Guidance is available within editorial segments of stories and productions. Teams can submit content for review and track the status of guidance requests.

## Sensitive Information

As legal and compliance details can be sensitive, the guidance system includes appropriate security controls and access restrictions to protect confidential information.
