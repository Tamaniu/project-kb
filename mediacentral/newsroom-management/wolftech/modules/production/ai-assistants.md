# AI Assistants

## AI Principles

Wolftech is dedicated to harnessing AI to enhance its public facing newsroom capabilities while maintaining the highest standards of journalistic integrity and responsibility.

The implementation of AI within Wolftech is guided by a commitment to responsible AI practices aligned with the media industry's specific challenges, such as maintaining trust, protecting copyrights, and ensuring accountability in automated content processes.

## Responsible AI Implementation

AI features within Wolftech are designed to:
- Enhance productivity and efficiency for editorial teams
- Support content creation and editorial decision-making
- Maintain transparency about AI involvement in workflows
- Protect creator rights and intellectual property
- Ensure human oversight of critical editorial decisions

## AI-Powered Features

Wolftech integrates AI capabilities across various workflows to help teams:
- Analyse and categorise content
- Suggest tags and metadata based on content
- Support editorial workflows with intelligent assistance
- Enhance search and discovery capabilities
