# Avid Content Core — Connecting and Signing In

**Source:** docs.avid.com ACC Help — Connecting to Avid Content Core  
**Extracted:** 01/05/2026 | Docs version: 0.0.3

---

## Browser Requirements

ACC requires **Google Chrome** only. The following are explicitly not supported:

- Microsoft Edge
- Apple Safari
- Any other Chromium-based browser
- Mobile web browsers (on mobile devices)
- Portable versions of Chrome (not fully installed on a workstation)

Minimum screen resolution: **1280 × 1024 pixels**. Some UI elements may not display below this.

Chrome extensions are not tested with ACC and may introduce performance impact affecting playback.

**Note on graphics drivers:** Some graphics driver features may affect asset playback. If playback issues occur, try lowering video resolution or increasing display refresh rate.

---

## Signing In

1. Open Google Chrome and navigate to your ACC URL. The Sign In window appears.  
   *(If you don't know your ACC URL, contact your local administrator.)*
2. Enter your credentials. Depending on your organisation's configuration, this may include standard username/password or a 2-factor/additional security verification step.
3. **(First sign-in only)** The Experience Personalisation window appears:
   - **Consent** — allows Avid to collect usage data to enhance your ACC experience (improves Landing Page recommendations).
   - **Decline** — opts out of data collection. Landing Page asset recommendations will be much less personalised; only group-level trends are shown.
   - This preference can be changed at any time in **User Settings > Data Collection**.

On first sign-in, the Landing Page app is displayed by default. On reconnection, the last app used is displayed.

---

## Signing Out

Avid recommends always signing out at the end of a session to ensure the connection is securely closed.

1. Click the **User Menu** in the upper-right corner.
2. Click **Sign Out**.
