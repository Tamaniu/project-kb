## Additional Properties in MediaCentral Search Connector

This release introduces additional properties to support the MediaCentral | Cloud UX v2025.6 Wolftech News integration and the Avid Ada Transcribe Speech-to-Text (STT) functionality with Production  Management through MediaCentral Search.

Note - The “MediaCentral Search Connector” window under the Production Management Administrator Client v2024.10.3 must be launched once in order to register these properties to the Production Search Connector used in Wolftech and STT workflows.
