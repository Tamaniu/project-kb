## Key Observations for CSM Use

- **MCNM v2024.10 is the only LTM with active development support** (End of Dev/Support: 30/11/2027).
- **MCNM v2023.7 End of Support is 31/07/2025** — customers on this version are already past End of
  Development and will lose support within months of this document's last-verified date.
- **All MCNM v2020.x–v2021.x versions** (the versions being processed in this KB batch) reached
  **End of Support on 30/06/2024** and are no longer supported.
- **All MCNM v2017.x–v2019.x** reached End of Support on **30/03/2024**.
- **MediaCentral v2022.3 LTM** reached End of Support on 30/06/2024.
- The current active LTM cycle for MediaCentral is **v2024.10** (to Nov 2027).
- **NEXIS** follows a different cadence — rolling annual releases, ~3 years support window.
- **Media Composer** LTM also releases annually in December, ~2 years support per release.
